Turn Lab is a ski instruction app that teaches skiing technique through structured skills and mental cues.

The app includes an AI Coach feature (Johnny or Paige) powered by OpenAI that provides personalized skiing advice.

FREE FEATURES:
- All 5 Beginner skills with full content
- Assessment quiz and progress tracking
- Focus Skill feature
- AI Coach (5 messages/day limit)

PREMIUM ($9.99):
- All 20 skills across 4 levels
- Unlimited AI Coach messages
- Home Screen Widget

No demo account is required - the app does not require login.
