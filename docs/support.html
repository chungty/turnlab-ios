Frequently Asked Questions

How do I assess my skill level?

Navigate to any skill in the Skills tab, then tap "Assess" to rate your current ability. Use the milestone descriptions to guide your self-assessment honestly.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes all Beginner level skills with videos, tips, drills, and full assessment functionality.

What does the premium unlock include?

The one-time premium purchase ($4.99) unlocks all Novice, Intermediate, and Expert level skills - a total of 15 additional skills with comprehensive content.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Is my data backed up?

Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you back up your iPhone through iCloud or iTunes, your Turn Lab data will be included in the backup.

Can I use Turn Lab offline?

Yes! All skill content, tips, drills, and checklists work offline. Only video playback requires an internet connection.
