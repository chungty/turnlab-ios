Got Questions?

Here's the scoop on Turn Lab.

What is Turn Lab?

Turn Lab is an iOS app that teaches you to ski better through 20 structured skills and mental cues that stick. Your coaches Johnny and Paige guide you from pizza wedge to powder skiing.

Is Turn Lab really free?

Yes! All 5 beginner skills are completely free with full content including mental cues, videos, and drills. Premium ($4.99 one-time) unlocks all 20 skills.

Who are Johnny and Paige?

They're your ski coaches in the app. Johnny is chill and encouraging, Paige brings the energy and motivation. Pick your favorite or switch anytime.

What are mental cues?

Mental cues are vivid images that help you remember technique on the slopes. Instead of "apply inside edge pressure," we say "imagine your ski is a pizza slice." These stick with you on the chairlift when you need them!

Do I need any special equipment?

Nope! Just an iPhone (iOS 17+) and skis. Turn Lab focuses on teaching technique through mental cues and progression tracking. No sensors, no gadgets, no accessories needed.

Is there a subscription?

Never! Turn Lab has no subscription. Free users stay free forever. Premium is a one-time $4.99 purchase that unlocks everything forever, including all future updates.

How does Turn Lab compare to ski lessons?

Turn Lab complements real ski lessons‚Äîit doesn't replace them. Use it to prepare before lessons, remember what your instructor taught, and practice between ski trips. The mental cues help you retain technique when you're not on the slopes.

What skill levels does Turn Lab cover?

Turn Lab covers 4 skill levels: Beginner (pizza wedge, basic stance), Novice (wedge christie, traverse), Intermediate (parallel turns, carving basics), and Expert (moguls, powder, steep terrain). 20 skills total across 5 domains.
